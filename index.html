Simon

Strict Mode

Instructions

  1. Click Start. A coloured button will be highlighted and its sound will play.
    • If you want the game to force you to start over if you make a mistake, click the Strict Mode checkbox before clicking Start.
  2. Click the colour that was highlighted. If you click the wrong button, an error sound will play and you will be prompted to try again (normal mode) or start a new game (strict mode). If you click the correct button, a bell will sound, your score will increase by 1 and the same button plus one more will be highlighted.
  3. Click the coloured buttons that were highlighted, in the order in which they were highlighted. The sequence of highlighted colours will increase by 1 with each round.
    • If you want to cancel the current game and start a new one, click Reset and then Start.
  4. Continue clicking the highlighted sequences of colours until you reach the maximum score of 20.
